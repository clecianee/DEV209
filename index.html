Twin Falls

Easy/Moderate

Enjoy a nice walk alongside the river with some steps and beautiful mossy trees. Great four-season trail with very little obstacles and nice waterfalls.

Annette Lake

Moderate

Fair length trail with a good elevation gain. This trail is perfect for spring and summer. It has plenty of tree-covered areas, as well as many spots for overnight camping.

Snow Lake

Moderate/Hard

One of the best hiking trails with a stunning lake at the end. It gets pretty busy in the summer but the whole path is worth it the effort.
